## AI Implementation Reality vs. Hype

[Gary Marcus](https://x.com/GaryMarcus/status/2059854118060257638) and others are documenting a persistent gap between AI demos and real-world deployment. Marcus highlights how controlled environments with clean inputs fail catastrophically when deployed to messy real-world conditions—citing examples from pizza-topping robots ($250K losses) to Starbucks' inventory system that hallucinated non-existent objects. [Palantir CEO Alex Karp](https://x.com/GaryMarcus/status/2059610429962531164) frames this as "the appearance of software working is not software working," warning against conflating AI slop with actual functionality. This pattern suggests significant ROI challenges ahead for enterprises making large capital commitments.
